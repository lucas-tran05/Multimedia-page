ABOUT PROGRAM

Anticipating the demands of the Industry 4.0 era, PTIT officially launched its Multimedia Communication undergraduate program in 2015 — becoming
the first university in Vietnam to offer formal training in this field. Here, you’ll master cutting-edge technologies to create impactful media products for a rapidly evolving digital world.

EXHIBITION & TALK SHOW – REFLECTING ON 100 YEARS OF REVOLUTIONARY JOURNALISM: FROM TRADITION TO MODERNITY

The celebration served as a moment to honor the proud legacy of Vietnam's revolutionary journalism and featured insights from veteran journalists on the challenges of media in the digital age. A parallel photo exhibition took viewers on an emotional journey through the history of revolutionary journalism - from wartime reporting and the subsidy era to the rise of modern journalism.
